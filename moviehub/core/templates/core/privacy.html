Privacy Policy

MovieHub is a demo application. We care about user privacy. Below is a short summary of how data is used.

Personal data

We store only the information you provide during registration (username, email) for authentication. You can delete your account via the admin interface.

Watchlist & reviews

Watchlist entries and reviews you create are stored in the local database so they can be shown on your account pages. This data is not shared.

Third-party services

We use TMDb for movie data. For any third-party services, refer to their privacy policies.

Contact

If you have questions about privacy, contact the site administrator.
